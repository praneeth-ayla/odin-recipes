Lemonade

Description:

Who doesn't love a cool glass of lemonade on a hot day? Here's our easy method for perfect lemonade every time, with simple syrup and fresh lemon juice.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sugar (can reduce to 3/4 cup)
  • 1 cup water (for the simple syrup)
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 to 3 cups cold water, to dilute

Steps:

  1. Make the simple syrup
  2. Juice the lemons
  3. Mix the lemonade
  4. Chill and serve
