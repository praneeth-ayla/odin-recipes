chai

Description

It is half a portion of chai/tea. Its served in special kind of glasses instead of cups.

Ingredients

  • tea leaves
  • ginger
  • cardamom powder
  • milk

steps

  1. 1. Keep all the ingredients ready for making the cutting chai.
  2. 2. Crush 2 to 3 green cardamoms in a mortar-pestle.
  3. 3. Crush till you get a semi-fine or coarse powder.
  4. 4. Heat 2 cups water in a vessel or pan.
  5. 5. Now add the grated or minced ginger
  6. 6. Simmer for 2 to 3 minutes on a medium heat.
  7. 7. Add the crushed cardamom powder.
  8. 8. Boil for 1 to 2 minutes more.
  9. 9. Add 2 teaspoons of Assam tea powder or black tea powder.
  10. 10. Boil the tea on a medium heat for 3 to 4 minutes or as needed.
  11. 11. Pour ¼ to ½ cup of milk. If you like strong tea then add ¼ cup of milk. If you like more milk then add ½ cup.
  12. 12. Continue to simmer the tea for 1 to 2 minutes on medium heat.
  13. 13. Switch off the heat, strain the tea onto a mesh strainer kept above the serving glasses or cups.
  14. 14. Serve the cutting chai hot along with some tea time snacks like nankhatai, pakora or samosa or murukku or sandwich or biscuits.
